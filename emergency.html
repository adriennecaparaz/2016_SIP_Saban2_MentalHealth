Suicide

The risk of suicide is greater if a behavior is new or has increased and if it seems related to a painful event, loss, or change.

Suicide Hotline: 800-784-2433
Immediate Medical Assistance: 911
Crisis Call Center: 800-273-8255 or text ANSWER to 839863
Talk to someone online

If you or someone you know is feeling suicidal seek help as soon as possible by calling the Lifeline at 1-800-273-TALK (8255)
